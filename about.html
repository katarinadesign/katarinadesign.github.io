I am a Graphic Design student of Miami International University of Art and Design. I was born in Republic of Serbia on August 30th, 1990. My passion for art started while I was 6 years old when I started drawing and painting and I continued exploring while being in primary school, secondary and high-school. I got the opportunity to study art when I moved to Miami, since doing it in my country was impossible due to the circumstances. I would love to learn as much as I could about design, I believe it is an enormous field that can never be explored enough!Now my focus is on layout design, branding, drawing, typography, photography and lettering.
